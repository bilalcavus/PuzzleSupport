About the Game

Puzzle Game is a simple, relaxing puzzle experience designed to keep your mind active. Solve levels, improve your logic, and enjoy quick sessions anytime.

Tip: Keeping the app updated ensures you get the latest levels, fixes, and performance improvements.

Quick Help

  • App crashes or won’t open: Update the app, restart your device, and try again.
  • Stuck on a level: Try different strategies, take a short break, or look for hidden patterns.
  • Ads or rewards not working: Check your internet connection and reopen the app.
  • Sound not playing: Check device silent mode and in-game sound settings.

FAQ

  • Do I need an account? No. Puzzle Game does not require an account to play.
  • Does the game work offline? Core gameplay usually works offline, but ads/rewards may require internet.
  • How do I reset my progress? If the app includes a “Reset Progress” option, you can find it in Settings. Otherwise, reinstalling may remove local progress.
  • Can I suggest new levels or features? Yes. Send us your ideas via email.

Troubleshooting

  • Performance issues: Close other apps and free up device storage.
  • Game freezes: Force close the app and reopen it.
  • Purchase issues (if applicable): Make sure you are signed in to the correct store account and try “Restore Purchases”.

When contacting support, please include: device model, OS version, app version, and a short description of what happened.
